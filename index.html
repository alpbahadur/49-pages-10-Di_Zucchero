Where Culinary Art Meets Entertainment

Welcome to Di Zucchero, Condado's premier destination for authentic Italian dining and vibrant nightlife. Located on the iconic Ashford Avenue, we've created a unique dual experience that celebrates the best of both worlds.

By day and evening, our restaurant serves handcrafted pizzas, fresh pasta, and traditional Italian dishes in an intimate, stylishly designed space. As night falls, La Piazza Terrace Club comes alive upstairs, transforming into one of San Juan's most exclusive nightlife destinations.

From our warm hospitality to our commitment to quality, Di Zucchero embodies the Italian philosophy of "la dolce vita" ‚Äì the sweet life. We're proud to be pet-friendly and welcome you to experience the magic of Italy in the heart of Puerto Rico.
